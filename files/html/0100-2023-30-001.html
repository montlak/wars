ABOUT THE AUTHOR

Peter Broida, who practices law and resides in Arlington, Virginia, graduated from New York University Law School in 1973. Through 1977, Mr. Broida represented corporate and union clients in private sector labor relations cases. From 1977 until 1980, he was employed as counsel to the American Federation of Government Employees. In 1980 he returned to private practice to provide representation to employees, agencies, and unions before the MSPB, FLRA, EEOC, labor arbitrators, and the Federal Circuit. Mr. Broida's web page is www.civilservicelaw.com.
