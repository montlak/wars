broida mspb Chapter 15: Attorney Fees Chapter 15: Attorney Fees, Subchapter 02: Procedures for Obtaining Fees, Section C: Reasonableness of Fees, Subsection 06: Examples of Fee Awards, Part a: Rates
a. Rates

$50/hour: Piper v. DOJ, 3 MSPR 571, 574, 4 MSPB 90 (1980).

$70/hour: McBride v. USDA, 3 MSPR 495, 498, 4 MSPB 17 (1980).

$80/hour: Weaver v. Dept. of Army, 29 MSPR 565, 569 (1985); Erdman v. Dept. of Labor, 6 MSPR 51, 54, 6 MSPB 54 (1981) (supporting the proposition that $70-90 per hour is the normal fee range for attorneys in Washington with experience in the civil service field).

$90/hour: Coltrane v. Dept. of Army, 32 MSPR 6, 10 (1986); Broadnax v. USPS, 28 MSPR 528, 530 (1985).

$100/hour: Ceja v. DLA, 34 MSPR 399, 404-05 (1987); Tri v. SEC, 33 MSPR 1, 3-4 (1987) (also approving $95 per hour for the time of an associate); Gavette v. OPM, 31 MSPR 115, 118 (1986); Logan v. DHUD, 23 MSPR 345, 348 (1984).

$110/hour: Crumbaker v. Dept. of Labor, 24 MSPR 627, 630 (1984).

$125/hour: Dishman v. USPS, 37 MSPR 295, 297 (1988); $125 for compliance proceedings: Spezzaferro v. FAA, 32 MSPR 643, 651 (1987); Alexander v. Dept. of Navy, 28 MSPR 569, 571 (1985) ($125 for a partner; $100 for an associate); McKinney v. Dept. of Air Force, 26 MSPR 267, 270 (1985) (a rate of $125 per hour was awarded to counsel who had received that rate, as well as a rate of $90 per hour, in past Board cases); Coleman v. Dept. of Transp., FAA, 21 MSPR 596, 606 (1984) ($125 per hour for hearing time; $85 per hour for nonhearing time; an associate's compensation: $40 per hour); Losure v. ICC, 18 MSPR 388, 392 (1983) ($125 an hour for a partner in a Washington law firm whose regular billing rate ranged from $125-200/hour).

$135/hour: SSA v. Balaban, 33 MSPR 309, 318-20 (1987).

$150/hour: Key v. USPS, 33 MSPR 142, 151 (1987).

$175-$225/hour: Rothschild v. DHUD, 54 MSPR 238, 243 (1992).

$200/hour: Rhea v. OPM, 59 MSPR 254, 259 (1993) (an appeal from a denial of disability retirement: 105 hours).

$250/hour: Lynch v. Dept. of Educ., 65 MSPR 407, 412-13 (1994).

$300/hour: Krape v. DOD, 97 MSPR 430 (2004).

$300/hour: Hart v. Dept. of Transp., 115 MSPR 10, 2010 MSPB 183 (2010).

$340/hour: Coradeschi v. DHS, 109 MSPR 591, 595 n.3, 2008 MSPB 199 (2008).

$350/hour: McKenna v. Dept. of Navy, 108 MSPR 404, 2008 MSPB 69 (2008).

$450/hour: Kilpatrick v. VA, CB-7121-13-0181-A-1 (NP 1/16/2015).

$505/hour: Tanner v. DOD, DC-0752-12-0209-A-1 (NP 8/1/2014) (structured fee agreement calling for Laffey Matrix rates and surveying initial decisions awarding fees in the DC Metro area).
