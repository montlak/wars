Adjutant Gen., Vt., Vt. Air Nat'l Guard and Green Mountain Chap., ACT, 5 FLRA 779, 779-87 (1981) (supervisory status of GS-1102-9 Procurement Agent; GS-504-09 Budget and Accounting Analyst; GS-501-06 Supervisory Pay Technician; GS-1702-08 Training Technician; GS-699-09 Health Technician).

Div. of Military and Naval Affairs, Nat'l Guard Bureau and ACT, N.Y. State Council, 13 FLRA 247, 248-50 (1983) (management and supervisory status of GS-7/9 Health Technician, Personnel Technician, Security Officer).

Mississippi Army Nat'l Guard and ACT, 57 FLRA 337, 342 (2001) (appropriate unit included the Mississippi Army National Guard; unit was not required to also include Mississippi Air National Guard; agency did not establish that MSARNG and MSANG technicians were so fully integrated that they lacked separate community of interest).

New Jersey DOD and Nat'l Army-Air Technicians Ass'n, Local 371, 7 FLRA 409, 409-11 (1981) (unit clarification: 107th Air Refueling Group and 108th Consolidated Work Area).

Pa. Air Nat'l Guard and Pa. State Council, ACT, 13 FLRA 538, 538-40 (1983) (supervisory and management status of GS-13 Flight Instructors, Navigator).
